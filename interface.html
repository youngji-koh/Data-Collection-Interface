How to use!

Step 1. Choose the category

Step 2. Mark points on a picture according to the outline of the object

Step 3. Fill in the blank with thd object's description

Step 4. Click the "Finish" button when you finished marking one polygon

Step 5. If you mark an wrong point, you can "Undo" or "Reset" the points

Step 6. If you want to start other objects, click "Resume" button

Step 7. If you finish marking all polygons, click "Submit" button
